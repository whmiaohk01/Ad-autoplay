This player does the following:

  1. Iphone's ios version should below ios17,mine is ios15.4
  2. Wait the pre scroll play done
  3. When content video playing, click mute button to unmute the video
  4. The mid-roll ad will only show less then 1s and then the content video continue play
  5. If we do not unmute the video in step2, the mid-roll ad will show as normal
  6. If I comment the code this.vjsPlayer.on('volumechange', this.onVolumeChange.bind(this)); in ima.js. The mid-roll ad can play if I unmute the video
